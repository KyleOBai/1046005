Undertale: A tale of determination


Description

Undertale is a jrpg inspired game created by Toby Fox(Code and Composition) and Temmy Chang(Artist). The Game is about a child(supposedly named chara) of no specific gender who goes exploring as he questions himself/herself. On his way he trips over and falls into a deep pit.

The main character then finds themself in an interesting adventure with many moral dilemmas, challenges and many potential new friends or foes.
